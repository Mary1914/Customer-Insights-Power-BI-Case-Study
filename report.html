Customer Insights – Report

This page shows dashboard screenshots + the insight analysis.

Dashboard 1: Insights Overview (KPIs + Churn by State + Calls)

  • Churn varies by location (state clusters visible on the map).
  • Customer service calls are a strong churn signal — churned customers show higher call activity.

Dashboard 2: Payment Method + Contract Category

  • Monthly contract customers show much higher churn than yearly.
  • Longer average account length aligns with lower churn.

Dashboard 3: Extra Charges & Usage

  • Higher extra charges (international/data) correlate with higher churn.
  • Bill surprises are a retention risk — pricing transparency matters.

Dashboard 4: Unlimited Plan vs Churn (Grouped Consumption)

  • Customers without unlimited plans churn more, especially in low usage groups (<5GB).
  • Unlimited plans reduce churn and stabilize retention across usage ranges.

Results

  • Highest churn risk = Monthly contract + No unlimited plan + Extra charges + High service calls.
  • Yearly contracts and unlimited plans support stronger retention.

Recommendations

  • Promote upgrades from monthly to yearly contracts (discounts/loyalty offers).
  • Push unlimited plans to customers showing churn risk, especially low-usage groups.
  • Reduce bill-shock: improve billing transparency, alerts, and charge caps.
  • Use service-call volume as an early warning trigger for retention outreach.
  • Investigate high-churn states for service quality/pricing inconsistencies.

Conclusion

This dashboard clearly links churn to contract type, pricing pressure (extra charges), and service friction (support calls). The insights support targeted retention strategies and improved plan design.
