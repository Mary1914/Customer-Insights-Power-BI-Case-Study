Customer Insights – Power BI Analysis

Dashboard 1 – Overview & KPIs

High churn is associated with monthly contracts and higher customer service calls.

Dashboard 2 – Payment & Contract Analysis

Monthly contract customers show significantly higher churn compared to yearly contracts.

Dashboard 3 – Extra Charges & Usage

Customers with higher extra charges (data/international) churn more frequently.

Dashboard 4 – Unlimited Plan Impact

Unlimited data plans reduce churn across all consumption groups.

Conclusion

  • Monthly contracts are the highest churn risk
  • Unlimited plans improve retention
  • Billing transparency is critical
